About

Olga Kunarni Santoso formerly worked in Law Office K. Santoso (1993-2012). During her time there she specialized in the area of corporate, commercial and family law. She managed to receive her licenses as a sworn translator for legal documents (from Indonesian to English) and as an Intellectual Property in the years 2005 and 2006. With the senior partners preparing to retire in 2012 she decided to start her own law venture.

Her current law office specializes in intellectual property matters such as trademarks, patent, copyrights and industrial design. She is still able to translate legal documents upon request. She has exceptional communication in written English which is essential to winning the trust of the clients since she addresses most of her clients through email. She also ensures that client's inquiries are quickly addressed while making sure her responses are as reliable and correct as possible.
