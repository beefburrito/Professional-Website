About

I am a licensed IP consultant in Indonesia since 2006. Aside from that I am a licensed litigator before the courts of Indonesia and a licensed sworn translator for legal documents (from Indonesian to English). I am also registered as a legal consultant for financial services industry. I am assisted by a paralegal and an IT administrator. Aside from running my IP office, I am active in the Indonesian IP Attorney's ("AKHKI") the head of government and other institutions relations.

Being a solo practictioner, I offer a personal service that is responsive to client requirements at a reasonable price. Additionally I have over 25 years experience in handling IP and my background as a corporate legal counsel provides a value added for my corporate clients.
